Ergonomics is the science of designing workplaces to fit the human body. It focuses on creating work environments that match our physical capabilities. By implementing ergonomic solutions, we can prevent work-related pain and injuries, especially those caused by Musculoskeletal Disorders (MSDs).

This course will help identify workplace risk factors that can lead to MSDs. Learners will gain insights for creating a healthier work environment for themselves and their employees. By the end of this course, you will be able to identify and understand ergonomic risk factors in your workspace, proactively address potential problems, and implement effective solutions.

The course is developed in line with OSHA and NIOSH ergonomic guidelines.

While this course provides a strong foundation on ergonomics safety, consulting a certified ergonomist might be necessary for highly specialized applications.
